1 Introduction

Terms and Conditions, often abbreviated as T&C or TOS (Terms of Service), are a legal document that outlines the rules, guidelines, and contractual obligations that govern the relationship between a company or service provider and its users or customers. The introduction section of these terms typically provides an overview of the document's purpose and key points. It may include information about the company or service, a statement about the user's agreement to adhere to the terms, and an explanation of the document's structure. The introduction sets the tone for the rest of the Terms and Conditions and serves as the starting point for users to understand their rights and responsibilities when using a particular service or product.
